Retrospective Chart Review

  • Lower evidence level than Randomized Controlled Trials (RCT)
  • Much much cheaper and easier
  • Very important part of clinical medical research

Retrospective Chart Review

  • Report outcome data "as-is"
  • Compare treatment response
  • Identify possible prognostic factors

Selecting Patients (Easy)

  • Certain diagnosis
  • Treated at certain department

Selecting Patients (Hard)

  • Patients with certain diagnosis AND underlying/history
  • Patients with good/poor response to treatment
  • "That patient with the strange mass in his lungs"

Tag Based System

  • Still your EMR charts
  • Annotate your charts with searchable tags (like post-it notes)
  • Minimal change to workflow

Workflow

  • Clinical duties as usual
  • Import EHR into system
  • "Tag" important concepts or results
  • Search
  • Automated queries

Clinical Duty

  • Use existing EHR system
  • No need to modify anything
  • (Optional) Use ##hashtag inside EHR

Import

  • Institution-specific import filters
  • Import EHR, HIS (labs), PACS (image)
  • (Future) Appending new records to old records

Tag

  • Physician tags important concepts or results
  • GOLD STANDARD
  • Searchable and computable!
  • (Future) "meta-tags"
  • (Future) Ontology of tags
  • (Future) Logical processing of tags
  • (Future) Various tools to aid tagging
  • (Future) Ownership and collaboration of tags

Vision

Physicians believe that insufficient time is the greatest barrier to point-of-care learning and that efficiency is the most important determinant in selecting an information source.

Be the most efficient, relevant, and practical information source!
